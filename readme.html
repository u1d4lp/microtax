Microtax

Microtax is an education app for analysing the effect of taxes and subsidies on price. Besides printing plots representing the supply and demand curves, before and after taxation, it also calculates the tax share customers and suppliers pay each.

Requirements

Microtax is written on Python 3.7, and requires the following libraries in order to be interpreted:

  • tkinter
  • random
  • scipy
  • numpy,
  • and matplotlib
